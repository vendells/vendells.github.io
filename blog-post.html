22 recipes for men only (part 2)

The problem with potency overshadows all other problems in the health of men (I know by myself). But how to make it so that it would always be there? I left the tablets in principle, and collected recipes for you to help solve this problem problem. 3 parts, links to the missing one will be at the end of each post.

Maral root
With impotence, alcoholism. For a glass of water, 1 tablespoon of chopped roots, boil for 30 minutes, then infuse for 4 hours, strain. Use 3-4 times a day for 1 tablespoon of warm milk drink's.

Milkweed Palassa
Infusion. With impotence in men, mastopathy in women. Take 1 teaspoon of milkweed palassa and pour a glass of boiling water, insist all night, in the morning take a divided into 3 parts of the infusion (75 ml). The course lasts 3-4 weeks, gradually increasing the amount of up to 6 tablespoons of the infusion.

Dandelion root
An infusion of dandelion roots. 2 teaspoons of dandelion roots are infused in a glass of cold water for about 8-9 hours. After that, strain the infusion of dandelion root and take a quarter of a glass four times a day, before meal.

White mistletoe
A decoction of mistletoe white: 1 tablespoon of leaves pour 200 ml (1 Cup) of water, heat for 20 minutes. on a small fire, insist for 30 minutes. Strain, squeeze out the remaining leaves. Take a decoction of mistletoe before eating for 30-40 minutes.

Rue fragrant
Collection of Rue, cumin, Melissa, Valerian. Prepare a collection of 2 parts of Rue, 2 parts of Valerian, 3 parts of Melissa, 3 parts of white mistletoe, 1 part of cumin seeds. 2 teaspoons of this mixture pour a quarter Cup of warm boiled water (non-hot). Keep the infusion 10 hours under the lid. Then take up to three cups a day, if necessary.

Sagan dayla
Tea from Sagan Dail, helps to increase metabolism; strengthen the immune system, vascular walls; get rid of toxins; restore the work of the heart and kidneys. The branches of the plant are cut into pieces of 1 cm open and fill with a glass of warm boiled water (you can use hot water), so that the water completely covers the raw material. Keep the infusion under the lid for several minutes. Take once a day in the morning on an empty stomach for a quarter of a Cup, you can add to the tea.

Willow leaf sorrel
Tincture (with vodka) for opisthorchiasis, giardiasis, and diseases of the stomach, appetite disorders, metabolism, obesity. Take the ivolistnaya sausage and pour 10 parts of vodka 40%. You can use 40% alcohol. Infuse 2 weeks in a darkened place and take 20 -30 drops (depending on body weight) before meals for 15 minutes. Course duration 2 hours months. After a month, you must take a 10-day break. You can make lotions with this tincture.
