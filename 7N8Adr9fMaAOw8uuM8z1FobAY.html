The influence of beer on the male body

Regular alcohol consumption changes the reproductive system of the genitals

Under the influence of alcohol, negative changes occur in the male reproductive system. Spermatozoa change their structure and become inactive. This is a direct cause of occurrence infertilities. Potency completely disappears with time. The patient thinks only about the next dosage of alcohol.

Alcohol can affect potency positively if used in small doses

In this case, testosterone enters the bloodstream for a short period of time. This is enough to to relax mentally. Here there is an increase in sexual desire, the person feels a certain relaxation.

Can beer increase the potency?

However, beer and a potentiality can be quite compatible. The main problem is that that there is currently a lot of low-quality beer. This beer leads to adverse consequences for the body. If the beer is brewed with natural ingredients and does not contain "any chemistry", and if you consume it in small quantities, nothing bad will happen. Quality beer contains hops, which is a repository of a large number of vitamins, minerals, and essential oils. One liter of quality beer a day provides the body with a 20% dose of vitamin B2 and 32% of vitamin B6. The calcium and magnesium contained in the drink reduce the likelihood of the formation of gallstones and kidneys. Keep in mind that beer, even high quality, increases appetite due to starch compounds. Thus, there is a danger of obesity, which can eventually lead to the development of diabetes.

The negative impact of beer on the potency

Speaking of using high-quality beer, it is recommended to consume no more than 1 liter per day. once a week. In this case, the negative impact on potency will not be. A particular danger lies in the adolescent dependence on beer, which increases the chances of erectile dysfunction several times at an early age (19-20 years). This is due to the fact that the body at this age has not yet adapted to alcohol. Therefore teenagers are not recommended to drink beer even in small quantities. Therefore, we can say that the influence of beer on potency can be both negative and positive: everything depends on the quality of the drink, the amount and culture of the consumer.
